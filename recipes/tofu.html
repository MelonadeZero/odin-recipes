Fried Tofu Nuggets

What do you normally think of when you hear the word tofu? Many think of a soft, tasteless, mushy square, almost like a disgusting bean gelatin. Tofu is so much more and has so much more potential when cooked correctly!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • 1 Tbsp Cornstartch
  • 3 oz Extra Firm Tofu
  • 2 Tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cut tofu into small individual blocks
  2. put tofu blocks into a seperate bowl of soy sauce
  3. Pour sesame oil into large skillet and heat
  4. mix nutritional yeast, salt, and cornstartch into a bowl
  5. One by one put tofu into cornstartch mix and cover on all sides
  6. Place tofu into skillet, cook every side of the tofu for 3-4 minutes each
  7. Put tofu into a bowl and use sauce of choice, enjoy!
