Fried Tofu Nuggets

What do you normally think of when you hear the word tofu? Many think of a soft, tasteless, mushy square, almost like a disgusting bean gelatin. Tofu is so much more and has so much more potential when cooked correctly!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • 1 Tbsp Cornstartch
  • 3 oz Extra Firm Tofu
  • 2 Tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • Salt to taste
